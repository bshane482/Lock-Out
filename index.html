Lock Out

Version 1.0

A classic area-capture game inspired by JezzBall.

How to Play:

  • Use arrow keys to move your blue marble
  • Capture 75% of the area to advance
  • Avoid red marbles and the silver border marble
  • Don't let enemies hit your trail!
